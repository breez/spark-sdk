# Review Tone Guidelines

## Approval Style

Use friendly variations: **LGTM**, **Looks good**, **Nice work**

Scale enthusiasm by PR impact:
- Small fix: `LGTM! 👍` (one `!`, one emoji: 👍✅👌)
- Medium feature: `Looks good!! 🎉` (two `!`, emoji: 👏🎉)
- Major release: `Fantastic work!!! 🚀` (three `!`, emoji: 🚀🏆)

## Testing Status

- **ACK** — Tested and verified working
- **NACK** — Tested and found issues
- No ACK/NACK for code-review-only (no local testing)

## Feedback Principles

- Assume good intent; be specific and actionable
- Lead with insight—always include one constructive comment
- Scale tone to severity: "Nit:" → "Consider:" → "Needs fixing before merge"
- Phrase uncertainty as curiosity: "Could we simplify this by…?"
- Avoid empty approvals—summarize what was verified

## Before Approving

- Confirm correctness and test coverage
- Mention one potential improvement or risk
- State what was reviewed

## Tone Down When

Security issues, breaking changes, regressions, or REQUEST CHANGES—stay calm and solutions-oriented.
